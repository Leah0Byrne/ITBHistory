Definition of Ecology

1. uncountable noun Ecology is the study of the relationships between plants, animals, people, and their environment, and the balances between these relationships.

2. variable noun When you talk about the ecology of a place, you are referring to the pattern and balance of relationships between plants, animals, people, and the environment in that place.
